Welcome to the Human Computer Interface study on password memorization and entry on mobile devices.

In the first part of this study we will gather some information on your base typing proficiency. To do this, you will be presented with a series of short phrases to enter. Please enter these phrases as quickly as possible in the space provided. After entering the phrase press the '>' button to move on to the next one.

The button will be located in the upper right corner of the screen on an iPhone or iPod touch, and below the text box on an iPad
